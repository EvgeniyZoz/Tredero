MetaTrader 4
Trading Platform

Simplicity with a punch!

MetaTrader 4 is one of the most popular trading platforms for traders of all levels of experience, with a well-deserved reputation for being exceptionally user-friendly and offering an extensive range of advanced trading tools. The popularity of the MetaQuotes MQL4 programming language has resulted in a thriving online community of auto-trading robot and EA (Expert Advisors) developers.

Trading From Charts, One-Click-Trading, a library of built-in Technical Indicators, and sophisticated Analytical Tools are all features of MT4.

On the MT4 platform, each asset may be shown in nine different time frames, allowing traders to hone in on specific price dynamics. Thirty built-in technical indicators and a library of twenty-four charting alternatives are included with the platform. At the same time, the MQL4 programming language's capabilities enable the construction of bespoke indicators for the platform to be reasonably simple.

MT4 Analysis Tools

Multiple trade execution modes are available on the MetaTrader 4 platform. Instant, Market, Pending, and Trailing Stops are examples of these, which allow traders to fine-tune their positions based on their specific trading and risk management techniques. Trade Alerts keep you up to date on all the latest market news, so you'll never have to worry about missing a trade opportunity again!

MetaTrader 4 is available as a desktop application for Windows and macOS, as well as a mobile application for iOS and Android, and a device-independent browser-based platform. You can open a trading position on your PC and close it on your mobile device since all versions of the platform sync smoothly. We don't believe in limiting your trading options at Tredero. Your trading style will determine the platform you choose to use.

With encrypted data flow between your client terminal and the Tredero trade servers, security is a crucial element of MT4. Your trading accounts are always safe with MT4 and Tredero!
