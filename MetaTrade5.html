MetaTrader 5
Trading Platform

Trading Suite for Advanced Users

For millions of successful traders across the world, MetaTrader 5 is the trading platform of choice. MetaTrader 5 is a multi-asset platform for trading Forex, CFDs, Exchange Instruments, and Futures. It has a complete set of advanced trading features as well as a wide range of fundamental and technical analysis tools.

MetaTrader 5 includes sophisticated Market Depth features that give traders precise information about the market liquidity of any of the platform's tradable assets. MetaTrader 5 supports two different order accounting types: netting for exchange markets and hedging for FX trading.

On MetaTrader 5, one-click trading is fully supported, as are sophisticated trade types such as Instant, Market, Pending, and Trailing Stops, allowing traders to trade their positions according to their specific trading methods and risk management preferences.

MT5 Analysis Tools

MetaTrader 5 enhances the already remarkable range of analytical tools available on the MT4 platform, providing traders with a comprehensive set of tools for in-depth price analysis and movement forecasts.

Traders can have up to 100 charts open on their screens at any given time, which is more than enough to keep track of your whole trading portfolio. MetaTrader 5 includes a charting system with twenty-one time periods spanning from sixty seconds to thirty days. This range of time frames enables significantly more comprehensive price and trend analysis than is possible in the retail trading marketplaces.

For extensive price research across all of your assets, the trading platform comes pre-loaded with eighty technical indicators and analytical tools. In addition, the MQL5 programming language makes it simple to develop third-party indicators. Additional technical indicators, both free and commercial, can be downloaded, and custom indicators can be purchased through a vast and active MQL5 programming community.

MetaTrader 5 is available in three versions:

  • A desktop version for Windows and macOS systems
  • A mobile version for iOS and Android devices
  • A browser-based version
