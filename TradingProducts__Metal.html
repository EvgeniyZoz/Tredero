Trade Metals

Metals are an extremely popular trading asset, having been one of the world's original currencies and the norm for government-issued money for hundreds of years. Precious metals like gold, silver, platinum, palladium, and even commodity metals like copper have a wide range of uses, from electronics to jewelry, and platinum and palladium can even be found in your car's catalytic converter.
