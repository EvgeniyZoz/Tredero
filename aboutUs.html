About Us

A licensed and regulated Forex and CFD broker, Tredero has gained an excellent reputation for offering a highly diverse lineup of tradable asset classes with outstanding trade execution on the award-winning MT4 and MT5 trading platforms. Our traders can invest in the most highly traded instruments from Forex Currency Pairs, Global Indices, Blue-Chip Stocks and multiple commodities, including Energies, Metals and Agricultural products.

We never limit your trading options, and all our trading platforms are available for desktop and mobile devices. We also offer access to a browser-based trading platform, perfect for anyone on a shared device or who doesn't want to install any software on their PC.

The Tredero trading environment represents a completely transparent approach to online trading with a suite of add-ons designed to help improve your trading skills and results. In addition, our traders have access to a comprehensive library of trading resources, including tutorials and multimedia presentations and Technical Analysis from the market leaders in the field, Trading Central.

We've built our reputation on providing the best possible combination of tradable assets, trading platforms and account add-ons. A Tredero trading account combines advanced technical features and old-world customer service, ideal for traders of all experience levels. So from complete beginner to experienced investor, your trading is in the very best of hands with Tredero!
