Overview

MT5 Analysis Tools

MetaTrader 5 improves on the already impressive set of analysis tools on the MT4 platform, equipping traders with a complete arsenal of analysis tools designed for extensive and detailed price analysis and movement forecasts.

Traders can keep up to one hundred charts open on their screens at any one time, more than enough to track your entire trading portfolio in detail. The charting system built-in to MetaTrader 5 comes with twenty-one times frames, ranging from sixty seconds to thirty days. This range of time frames allows for a level of price and trend analysis far more advanced than anything else in the retail trading markets.

The trading platform comes pre-loaded with eighty technical indicators and analytical tools for detailed price analysis across all your assets. Third-party indicators can easily be created using the MQL5 programming language. Additional technical indicators, both free and paid, can be downloaded, while custom indicators are available for purchase via a large and very active community of MQL5 programmers.

MetaTrader 5 comes in desktop versions for machines running the Windows and macOS operating systems, a mobile version for devices running iOS and Android, and a convenient browser-based version.
